Owlette Costume for 18-Month-Olds

Introducing the ultimate Owlette costume for baby girls, crafted for comfort, style, and safe play. Designed for 18-month-olds, this cozy outfit is perfect for sparking imagination while keeping your little one warm and secure.

Thoughtfully made from a soft combination of 100% polyester flannel and microfiber faux fur, the costume offers a luxuriously warm and comfortable fit. The padded anti-skid soles on enclosed feet ensure stability and safety during every playful adventure.

With easy access through Velcro dot fasteners at the center back and inseam snaps, changing diapers has never been simpler. This multi-themed design allows for endless role-play and creative exploration, making it ideal for a baby who loves to imagine.

Whether it's a nighttime stargazing session or a sunny outdoor playtime, this Owlette costume is built to last, made for active toddlers. The vibrant colors and playful patterns are sure to brighten your baby's day and help her feel like a true magical character.

  • Soft, warm 100% polyester flannel and microfiber faux fur

  • Padded anti-skid soles for added safety and comfort
